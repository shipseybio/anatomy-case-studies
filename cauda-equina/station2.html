Definitions

  • Dorsiflexion: Lifting the front of the foot upward.

Patient Overview

John presents with left foot drop and cannot perform dorsiflexion. He also demonstrates left quadriceps weakness and a diminished patellar reflex on the same side. These findings suggest involvement of spinal motor nerves at the lumbar level.

Task: Review the Case

Based on the information provided, analyze the patient's motor testing and reflex responses. Consider the role of spinal nerves in controlling motor movements and reflexes.
